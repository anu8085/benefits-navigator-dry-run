# Demo and Pitch Guide

## 30-second pitch
Benefits Navigator helps New Jersey families quickly understand which public benefits they may qualify for. It combines trusted Databricks data, agentic Claude reasoning, explainable rules, Lakebase app-state, and analytics so support organizations can both guide families and understand community needs.

## 3-minute demo flow
0:00–0:20 Problem: families do not know which benefits to apply for.
0:20–0:45 Solution: Databricks App + trusted data + agent + rules + Lakebase.
0:45–1:45 Live user journey: intake, follow-up questions, matched programs, action plan.
1:45–2:20 Persistence proof: show Lakebase row counts and feedback saved.
2:20–2:45 Analytics proof: show category/program demand query.
2:45–3:00 Closing: scalable social-impact navigator for families and nonprofits.

## Winning line
This is not just a chatbot. It is an agentic Databricks data app that uses trusted data, explainable rules, Claude reasoning, Lakebase state, and analytics to help families move from confusion to action.

## What not to overemphasize
Do not lead with SQLite. Say SQLite is only a local development fallback. Lakebase is the official persistent app-state layer.
